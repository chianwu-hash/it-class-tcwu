# 六年級英文 U1-R1 題庫
範圍：
- Unit 1：休閒活動與 free time
- Unit 2：夢想職業
- Review 1：Units 1-2 綜合應用
難度分配：
- 困難：5 題
- 中等：10 題
- 容易：5 題
說明：
- 題目與選項使用繁體中文說明，英文句型與關鍵詞保留英文
- 依課本範圍出題，不超出 U1-R1

## 容易

1. 問別人「你休閒時間喜歡做什麼？」應該怎麼說？
A. What do you want to be?
B. What do you like to do in your free time?
C. Where do you go after school?
D. How do you spell it?
答案：B

2. 問別人「你想成為什麼？」應該怎麼說？
A. What do you want to be?
B. What do you like to eat?
C. Do you want a book?
D. What are you doing?
答案：A

3. 下列哪一個是正確的休閒活動說法？
A. go camp
B. listen music
C. go hiking
D. play the hiking
答案：C

4. 「I want to be a writer.」的意思是什麼？
A. 我喜歡看書。
B. 我想成為作家。
C. 我想成為老師。
D. 我正在寫故事。
答案：B

5. 「Do you like to go camping in your free time?」最合理的回答是什麼？
A. Yes, I do.
B. I’m a police officer.
C. She likes to swim.
D. At school.
答案：A

## 中等

6. Emma says, “I like to go camping and listen to music in my free time.” 從這句話可知什麼？
A. Emma 想當警察
B. Emma 的休閒活動是露營和聽音樂
C. Emma 不喜歡戶外活動
D. Emma 正在學校上課
答案：B

7. 問「What does Tony like to do in his free time?」最適合用哪一種回答？
A. He likes to go swimming.
B. I want to be a writer.
C. Yes, he do.
D. She likes to shop.
答案：A

8. 下列哪一句文法正確？
A. She like to go hiking.
B. He want to be a teacher.
C. They like to go shopping.
D. Does Kelly likes to camp?
答案：C

9. 「Does Sally like to watch TV in her free time?」若答案為肯定，應怎麼回？
A. Yes, she does.
B. Yes, she do.
C. Yes, I does.
D. Yes, Sally like.
答案：A

10. 「What do you want to be?」最合適的回答是什麼？
A. I like to play basketball.
B. I want to be a police officer.
C. Yes, I do.
D. He wants to be a writer.
答案：B

11. 根據 Unit 2，哪一組職業都是課本範圍內的 dream jobs？
A. writer, police officer, basketball player
B. chef, pilot, nurse
C. singer, doctor, farmer
D. dancer, firefighter, vet
答案：A

12. 「Do you want to be a writer, Frank?」若 Frank 不想，最自然的回答是什麼？
A. No, I don’t.
B. No, he doesn’t.
C. No, I’m not.
D. No, I do.
答案：A

13. 下列哪一個對話最合理？
A. What do you like to do in your free time? / I like to go hiking.
B. What do you want to be? / I like to listen to music.
C. Do you like to go camping? / I want to be a writer.
D. Does Amy like to swim? / Yes, I do.
答案：A

14. 「He wants to be a police officer.」中的主詞如果改成 Jenny，句子應變成哪一個？
A. Jenny want to be a police officer.
B. Jenny wants to be a police officer.
C. Jenny wanting to be a police officer.
D. Jenny do want to be a police officer.
答案：B

15. 下列哪一句文法正確？
A. Does Kevin wants to be a writer?
B. Does Kevin want to be a writer?
C. Do Kevin want to be a writer?
D. Kevin do want to be a writer?
答案：B

## 困難

16. Kelly says, “I like to go camping in my free time, but I want to be a writer.” 這句話最能說明什麼？
A. 休閒活動和夢想職業一定相同
B. 一個人可以同時有不同的興趣和夢想
C. Kelly 其實不喜歡露營
D. writer 是一種休閒活動
答案：B

17. 下列哪一段自我介紹最完整且符合 U1-R1 範圍？
A. Hi, I’m Ben. I like to go hiking in my free time, and I want to be a teacher.
B. Hi, I’m Ben. He likes to go hiking and want to be a teacher.
C. Hi, I’m Ben. Do you like to go hiking in your free time?
D. Hi, I’m Ben. I want to be go hiking.
答案：A

18. 閱讀對話：`A: What do you want to be? B: I want to be a police officer.` 下列哪一句改寫正確？
A. He want to be a police officer.
B. He wants to be a police officer.
C. He like to be a police officer.
D. He wanting to be a police officer.
答案：B

19. 閱讀對話：`A: What does Mark like to do in his free time? B: He likes to go shopping.` 根據對話，下列何者正確？
A. Mark likes to go shopping in his free time.
B. Mark wants to be a police officer.
C. Mark likes to listen to music in his free time.
D. Mark doesn't like shopping.
答案：A

20. 閱讀短文：`Hi, I’m Amy. I like to listen to music in my free time. I want to be a teacher.` 根據短文，下列何者正確？
A. Amy likes to go camping.
B. Amy wants to be a police officer.
C. Amy likes to listen to music and wants to be a teacher.
D. Amy doesn’t like music.
答案：C
